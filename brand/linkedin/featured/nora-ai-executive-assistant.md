**Nora — An AI Executive Assistant That Never Sends as You**

An email-native AI executive assistant, in real use today. Forward a thread to your
private Nora address and she drafts a reply, summary, or analysis in your voice — you
send it yourself. Every command in is verified by DKIM against your registered address;
every action out (drafting, proposing a meeting, sharing a file) is gated behind your
explicit confirmation and sent only from Nora's own labeled identity. Built and deployed
end to end: FastAPI, Gmail/Microsoft Graph mailbox providers, a per-relationship
data-trust ladder from hosted to fully self-hosted, and a draft-acceptance metric
tracked in production.
