**Vitals — A Governed Medallion Lakehouse for Healthcare Data**

Takes messy health data — FHIR, claims, wearable streams, clinical notes — and turns it into three
things: analytics tables, an ML feature store, and a search index over the notes. Runs end-to-end
locally with one command, no cloud needed. Built on Databricks, dbt, Airflow, and PySpark, with proper
de-identification and data-quality checks baked in. Built partly to get good at the kind of data
problems a company like Sword Health deals with every day.
